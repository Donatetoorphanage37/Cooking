How to make cheese at home

There are many advantages in making your own cheese. Making your own cheese at home ensures the freshness of your product, and it is generally cheaper by volume to make yourself because the necessary ingredients are few. While there are many different varieties of cheese that you can make at home, the processes are all very similar.This is the most beautiful and genuinely delicious block of cheese I have ever seen and surprisingly it’s homemade!Follow these steps to make your own cheese at home.

Ingredients

Full fat milk
Lemon juice
Eno
ghee
milk
salt

Recipe

1.Heat 1 litre full fat milk on low flame until your finger can bear but feels hot too.
Add 1 tbsp of lemon juice and stir well. Repeat this step 2 more times (3 in total) and you’ll notice the milk curdle. It is not paneer but elasticity of cheese.
2.Cover for 2 minutes and strain the water. Squeeze the cheese ball to get rid kg excess water and keep it aside.
3.In a chota bowl, add 1/2 tsp eno, 1/2 tsp water and 1/4th tsp lemon juice and you’ll see the bubbles appear.
4.Add cheese pieces into a mixer jar, add 2 tbsp of ghee, 2.5 tbsp of milk, 1/4 tsp salt and blend it until smooth.
